# RF Microneedling in Amman: The Complete Guide to Results, Cost & What to Expect

RF microneedling has become one of the most requested treatments at ArtiZone — and for good reason. It is the most powerful non-surgical skin treatment currently available, combining two proven mechanisms in a single session to address acne scars, skin laxity, enlarged pores, and skin texture simultaneously.

If you are considering RF microneedling in Amman and want to understand exactly how it works, what results to expect, and whether it is right for your skin concerns, this guide covers everything.

## What Is RF Microneedling?

RF microneedling (radiofrequency microneedling) combines two established treatments:

1. **Microneedling:** Fine needles create controlled micro-channels in the skin, triggering the body's natural wound-healing response and stimulating collagen and elastin production.

2. **Radiofrequency (RF) energy:** RF energy is delivered directly through the needles into the dermis at precise depths. The heat triggers additional collagen contraction and synthesis — amplifying the microneedling effect significantly.

The combination is far more powerful than either treatment alone. Standard microneedling works primarily at the surface. RF microneedling delivers energy at 1.5mm, 2mm, and 3.5mm depths — remodelling collagen at multiple layers simultaneously.

## What Can RF Microneedling Treat?

RF microneedling is one of the most versatile aesthetic treatments available because it addresses multiple concerns at once:

### Acne Scars

This is where RF microneedling delivers its most dramatic results. The combination of micro-channel creation and deep RF energy triggers intense collagen synthesis that gradually fills and smooths depressed scars from within.

- **Ice pick scars:** Significant improvement with 4–6 sessions
- **Boxcar scars:** Excellent results — edges soften and depth reduces
- **Rolling scars:** Very good results — the wave-like texture smooths progressively
- **Mixed scarring:** Combination protocols address multiple scar types simultaneously

Typical improvement: **60–80% reduction in scar visibility** after a full course of treatment.

### Skin Tightening and Lifting

RF microneedling is highly effective for skin laxity — particularly for clients who have both laxity and texture concerns. The RF energy heats the dermis and triggers collagen contraction, producing a tightening and lifting effect on the face, neck, and body.

**Best areas:** Jawline, jowls, neck, under-eye area, décolletage, abdomen, arms.

### Enlarged Pores

Enlarged pores are caused by excess sebum production and loss of the collagen structure that keeps pore walls tight. RF microneedling addresses both: the RF energy tightens the pore walls and the collagen response improves the surrounding skin structure.

Most clients notice a significant reduction in pore size after 3–4 sessions.

### Crepey Skin and Skin Texture

Crepey skin — the thin, crinkled texture that develops on the neck, décolletage, and inner arms with age — responds very well to RF microneedling. The deep collagen remodelling thickens and firms the skin, reducing the crepey appearance.

### Stretch Marks

RF microneedling is one of the most effective treatments for stretch marks, particularly newer (red/pink) stretch marks. Older (white/silver) stretch marks also improve, though results are more modest.

### Fine Lines and Wrinkles

The collagen remodelling effect softens fine lines and wrinkles progressively over the treatment course. RF microneedling is particularly effective for fine lines around the eyes, mouth, and forehead.

## How Does a Session Work?

### Before the Session

A topical numbing cream is applied to the treatment area 30–45 minutes before the session. This ensures the treatment is comfortable — most clients describe the sensation as mild pressure and warmth rather than pain.

### During the Session

The RF microneedling device is moved systematically across the treatment area. The needles penetrate to the programmed depth while delivering RF energy. You will feel warmth and mild pressure. The session takes 45–75 minutes depending on the area being treated.

### After the Session

Immediately after treatment, the skin looks red and feels warm — similar to a mild sunburn. This is completely normal and is a sign that the treatment has worked. Mild swelling is common, particularly around the eyes.

**Downtime:** 2–4 days of redness and mild swelling. Most clients feel comfortable returning to work after 2–3 days with mineral makeup. Full healing is complete within 5–7 days.

## RF Microneedling vs. Standard Microneedling

Many clients ask whether standard microneedling is sufficient or whether the RF component is worth it. Here is an honest comparison:

**Standard microneedling:**
- Works at the skin surface (0.5mm–2mm depth)
- Stimulates collagen through wound-healing response
- Effective for mild texture, PIH, fine lines
- Minimal downtime (1–2 days)
- Lower cost per session
- Best for: maintenance, mild concerns, sensitive skin

**RF microneedling:**
- Works at multiple depths (1.5mm–3.5mm)
- Stimulates collagen through both wound-healing AND RF heat
- Significantly more effective for deep scars, laxity, and pores
- More downtime (2–4 days)
- Higher cost per session, but fewer sessions needed for equivalent results
- Best for: moderate-to-severe scarring, skin laxity, enlarged pores

**The verdict:** For acne scars, skin tightening, and enlarged pores, RF microneedling delivers meaningfully better results than standard microneedling. For mild texture concerns and maintenance, standard microneedling is a cost-effective option.

## Is RF Microneedling Safe for Darker Skin Tones?

Yes — this is one of the most important advantages of RF microneedling over laser treatments.

Laser resurfacing carries a real risk of post-inflammatory hyperpigmentation (PIH) in olive and darker skin tones, which are common in Jordan and the Arab world. RF microneedling bypasses this risk entirely because it does not target melanin — the RF energy heats tissue uniformly regardless of skin tone.

This makes RF microneedling the preferred treatment for acne scars and skin tightening in darker skin types, where laser alternatives carry higher risk.

## Aftercare: What to Do (and Avoid)

Proper aftercare significantly affects your results. Follow these guidelines after each session:

**Do:**
- Apply the prescribed post-treatment serum or moisturiser as directed
- Use a gentle, fragrance-free cleanser for the first week
- Apply SPF 50+ every morning without exception — UV exposure during healing can cause PIH
- Stay hydrated
- Sleep on a clean pillowcase

**Avoid for 48–72 hours:**
- Makeup (mineral makeup is acceptable after 24 hours if needed)
- Active skincare ingredients: retinol, vitamin C, AHAs, BHAs
- Swimming pools, saunas, steam rooms
- Strenuous exercise (increases blood flow and swelling)
- Direct sun exposure

**Avoid for 1 week:**
- Waxing or threading the treated area
- Other facial treatments

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is RF microneedling painful?**

With topical numbing cream applied 30–45 minutes before the session, most clients find RF microneedling very tolerable. The sensation is mild pressure and warmth. Sensitive areas (around the nose, upper lip) may feel more intense briefly. Pain is not a common description.

**Can I wear makeup after RF microneedling?**

We recommend avoiding all makeup for the first 24 hours. After that, mineral makeup (powder or mineral foundation) is acceptable. Avoid liquid foundation for 48–72 hours as it can block the micro-channels during healing.

**How long until I can go back to work?**

Most clients return to work after 2–3 days with mineral makeup. If you have an important event, allow 5–7 days for full healing.

**Can RF microneedling be combined with other treatments?**

Yes — combination protocols often deliver the best results. RF microneedling pairs well with:
- **PRP (platelet-rich plasma):** Applied immediately after microneedling, PRP accelerates healing and enhances collagen stimulation
- **LED red light therapy:** Reduces inflammation and accelerates healing between sessions
- **Chemical peels:** Used between microneedling sessions to address PIH and surface texture

**How long do results last?**

The collagen remodelling triggered by RF microneedling is permanent — the new collagen does not disappear. Natural ageing continues, but treated skin ages more slowly. Maintenance sessions (1–2 per year) preserve and extend results.
